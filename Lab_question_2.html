Value Picks! 10 stocks with below industry PE levels that can rally up to 64%

Money Making Ideas
Low PE stocks are considered as value stocks, which are best suited for long-term investment horizons. UPL, Apollo Tyres, M&M Financial Services and Power Finance Corporation are trading at lower PE than their respective industry PE, according to Trendlyne data. Meanwhile, these stocks belong to different industries and can rally up to 64%, Trendlyne data suggested.
